Ingredients

  • Maize(corn)
  • Beans
  • Potatoes
  • Kitunguu na nyanya
  • Dhania

Steps

  1. Wash the maize and beans
  2. Pour into a pressure
  3. Pour in water until about 3/4full
  4. Cook until soft then wash
  5. Kaanga kitunguu na nyanya
  6. Ikiiva ongeza viazi
  7. Ongeza mahindi na maharagwe
  8. Acha iive dakika kama 15 hivi alafu ongeza dhania
  9. Ikiwa tayari serve na avocado
